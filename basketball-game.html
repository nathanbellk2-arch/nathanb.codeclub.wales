How to Play:

Player 1 (Blue) - WASD

  • W/A/S/D - Move up/left/down/right
  • Q - Shoot (when you have the ball)
  • E - Steal (when near opponent with ball)
  • Shift - Sprint (faster movement)

Player 2 (Red) - Arrow Keys

  • ↑/←/↓/→ - Move up/left/down/right
  • K - Shoot (when you have the ball)
  • L - Steal (when near opponent with ball)
  • Ctrl - Sprint (faster movement)

🏀 Hold the ball and move to dribble • Get past your opponent to score • First to score or highest score when time runs out wins!
